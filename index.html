We believe internet access should be fast, easy, and affordable for everyone. Our platform lets you buy data for yourself or any number in seconds, with payments secured by Paystack.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used as your account identifier and for data delivery
  • Password — stored securely and never shared
  • Transaction data — processed securely by Paystack (we do not store card details)
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics to improve our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your UnlimData account
  • To process data plan purchases and activate subscriptions
  • To send transaction confirmations
  • To respond to your support inquiries
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only with Paystack (payment processing) and network providers (phone number only, to deliver your data plan).

All data is stored securely. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Payment information is handled entirely by Paystack and never touches our servers.

5. Cookies

We use minimal browser storage (localStorage) to keep you signed in and remember preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

  • Right to opt out of communications

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of UnlimData after changes means you accept the updated policy.
